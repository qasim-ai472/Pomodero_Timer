# 🌳 GrowFocus: Cultivate Your Concentration

In an era of endless scrolls, pings, and notifications, our most precious resource—our attention—is under constant siege. We live in a world designed to distract us, making the path to deep learning and skill mastery feel like an uphill battle. 

I built **GrowFocus** to turn the tide. Inspired by my own transformation using the Pomodoro Technique during college, I wanted to create more than just a countdown clock. I wanted to create a living representation of your discipline.

---

## 🍃 Why I Built This

The inspiration for GrowFocus began during my university years. I stumbled upon research regarding the **Pomodoro Technique**—25 minutes of focused work followed by a short break. The science was sound, but the application was life-changing. My retention soared, my burnout faded, and the "distraction loops" that used to steal my afternoons finally broke.

However, I realized that in today's world, the chance of learning important, complex skills is lower than ever. Our phones and devices are engineered to fragment our focus. I built this web application because I wanted to make the process of focusing feel "alive." 

**The philosophy is simple: Make focus visible.** When you can watch your effort grow from a tiny seed into a towering tree in real-time, you are less likely to abandon your work. Each session isn't just a number on a clock; it's a living thing you are nurturing.

---

## ✨ Features

* **Real-Time Botanical Growth:** Watch a seed sprout and mature into a full-grown tree, perfectly synced to your 25-minute session.
* **The Eternal Forest:** Every successful session "plants" your tree in a persistent digital forest. Your hard work doesn't vanish; it accumulates visually.
* **Accountability by Design:** If you break your session early, the tree never completes. It’s a gentle, visual reminder to stay the course.
* **Deep Work Stats:** Track your total focus hours, completed sessions, and daily streaks to see your progress over time.
* **Pure Craftsmanship:** Built with zero libraries. Just raw HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for a lightweight, lightning-fast experience.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Details

To keep the project lightweight and "pure," I opted against using external frameworks or heavy libraries:

* **SVG Animations:** The tree is a custom-coded SVG with 5 distinct growth stages.
* **CSS Keyframes:** Growth transitions are handled via CSS to ensure smooth, hardware-accelerated performance.
* **Vanilla JavaScript:** The logic for the timer, state management, and local storage (to save your forest) is all written in standard JS.

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1.  **Plant:** Click start to plant your seed.
2.  **Nurture:** Dive into your work. The tree will grow as the minutes pass.
3.  **Bloom:** When the chime sounds, your tree is fully grown and added to your forest.
4.  **Rest:** Take a 5-minute break. You've earned it.
